What I've learned in this flow

In this flow I learned:

- To make my own project.

- To think every little detail through before I start doing anything.

- To understand and use tools like PBS, WBS, wireframe, etc. independently (not in a group).

- To have a reason, an argument, for my design choices.

- To look at my projects and be critical about them - try to make an improvement.
